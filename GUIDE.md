# 📖 Trading Guide: How to Respect the Process

Getting a signal is only 50% of the work. The other 50% is your execution. This guide explains how to interpret the **Omni-Pro** Discord alerts.

### 1. Understanding the Alerts

#### 🧱 The "Build" Alert
* **What it means:** The market is "engineering" liquidity. This is not a trade signal.
* **Action:** Identify the level on your chart. **Do not** place your stop loss at this level. Wait for the market to "raid" this zone.

#### 🔥 The "Institutional Signal" Alert
* **What it means:** Phase 4 (Displacement) has occurred. The trap was sprung, and price has reversed with intent.
* **Action:** Check the Trade Plan provided (Entry, SL, TP).

### 2. The Execution Strategy
The bot provides an **Entry Price** based on the breakout of the Raid candle. For a higher-probability entry:
1. Look for a **Fair Value Gap (FVG)** on the chart near the Entry Price.
2. If the RRR (Risk-to-Reward Ratio) is above 2.0, consider the trade.
3. If the signal has a **Volume Confirmation** and **Killzone** checkmark, it is a high-conviction setup.

### 3. Risk Management
* **Stop Loss:** Always use the Stop Loss provided. It is placed behind the "Raid Wick." If that wick is broken, the institutional thesis is invalidated.
* **Take Profit:** The TP is set at the opposite side of the range where retail "Build" liquidity resides.
